Welcome to the Cirrhosis Prediction Tool

About This Tool

This is a powerful web-based tool designed to assist in understanding the potential progression of Cirrhosis. By leveraging a machine learning model, we analyze key medical and demographic data to provide an estimated health status. This tool serves as a valuable resource for patients and healthcare professionals alike, helping to inform discussions and monitor health trends.

How to Use

  1. Log in using your provided credentials below to gain access to the prediction form.
  2. Once logged in, you will be redirected to the prediction form page.
  3. Fill in the necessary medical data accurately and click "Predict Status".
  4. Receive a real-time prediction, including probabilities for the different health statuses.

Note: This tool is for educational and informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.
